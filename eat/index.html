Quick Answers

The questions visitors ask most often.

What is the best restaurant in Watch Hill?

It depends on the meal you want. For a special-occasion dinner, Coast at Ocean House. For lunch with character, the Olympia Tea Room. For casual waterfront dining, Bay Street Deli or the Watch Hill Inn's restaurant. For ice cream, St. Clair Annex with no debate.

Do Watch Hill restaurants take reservations?

Ocean House properties, Weekapaug Inn restaurants, and Bridge Restaurant in Westerly take reservations and need them in season. Olympia Tea Room is walk-in only and has waits at peak times. Casual spots and ice cream counters are first-come, first-served.

Are Watch Hill restaurants open year-round?

Most close from mid-October through April. Ocean House operates year-round with reduced restaurant hours. A handful of Westerly restaurants (like Bridge) stay open all year. Always call ahead in winter or shoulder season.

What's the dress code at Ocean House?

Smart casual is enforced — no flip-flops, no tank tops, no swimwear in restaurants or the verandah. Coast (the tasting menu restaurant) leans dressier in the evening. The Bistro is more relaxed. Resort-elegant is a safe bet.
