Quick one.

If you're at the "I'm thinking about a trip to Watch Hill" stage, almost every other article on the internet is going to give you the same 8 things — Ocean House, the carousel, the lighthouse, Olympia Tea Room, Napatree Point, etc. They're all real. They're all fine. But knowing the *list* isn't what makes a trip good.

What makes a trip good is knowing **when** to do each thing.

The carousel before 11am is a children's ride. The carousel at 4pm is a 90-minute line. Napatree before 9am is a private nature reserve. Napatree at 1pm is a beach towel mosaic. Same place. Different trip.

If you read nothing else on the site, read this one. Everything else (where to stay, where to eat, day trips) makes more sense once you've got the timing down.
