Last email in this welcome sequence. Here's what to expect from me going forward:

**Frequency:** One email a month, sometimes two if something time-sensitive is happening (a restaurant week, a hotel deal worth knowing about, peak foliage timing). Never more.

**Content:** Usually one of three things —
1. **A "right now" email** — what's worth doing in Watch Hill *this month* specifically. Different in March than June than November.
2. **A deep dive** — one place, one restaurant, one walk, told properly. Not a top-10 list.
3. **A heads-up** — when something I know about is happening (a rate drop, a new opening, an event worth driving for).

**What I won't do:** sell you a course, run a Black Friday sale, beg you to share with friends, ask you to "engage" with my content, or send you anything that I wouldn't be glad to receive myself.

**What I might do, eventually:** if I write a longer paid guide (a real one, full season-by-season, the kind that takes months), I'll let you know about it once. You can ignore it. The free emails won't change.
