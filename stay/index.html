Quick Answers

The questions visitors ask most often.

How much does Ocean House cost per night?

Rooms start around $800/night in peak season (June–August) and climb past $2,000 for suites. Shoulder-season rates (May, September, October) can drop to around $500. Off-season rates in winter can dip below $400. Always check directly — promotional rates appear regularly.

What's the best hotel in Watch Hill for couples?

Weekapaug Inn is the local favorite for couples — quieter than Ocean House, more intimate, and located on a private pond ten minutes east of the village. Both are Relais & Châteaux properties and guests can use amenities at either.

Are there cheap hotels in Watch Hill?

Not in the village itself — Watch Hill has three landmark hotels, all in the $400+/night range. For budget options, look in Westerly (10 minutes inland) or along the Misquamicut beach strip. VRBO and Airbnb tend to be the best value for families or groups.

When should I book a Watch Hill hotel for summer?

By February or March for prime July/August weekends. Memorial Day and Fourth of July weekends often sell out by April. Weekday stays in June and September can be booked much closer to the date with flexibility on price.
